M E N U

Dishes served throughout the day.

Breakfast

Waffles

Classic waffles with customizable ingredients, including cinnamon sugar.

Fruits: strawberry, blueberries.

Syrup: maple syrup, chocolate, strawberry.

Philly Cheesesteak Omelette

Cheesesteak with slices of American cheese and loaded with onions, jalapeno peppers, grilled tomatoes and grilled mushrooms.

Biscuits

Meat of choice: sausage; sausage, egg & cheese; bacon, egg & cheese; chicken (regular or spicy), served on a grilled biscuit.

Lunch

Cheeseburger

Classic cheeseburger served on a brioche bun.

Customizable ingredients include jalapenos, bacon, and pickles.

Melted mozzarella, cheddar, and Monterey Jack with jalapeno sauce.

Buffalo Wings

Classic chicken wings with choice of sauce or dip, boned-in or boneless.

Sauces and dip: honey barbecue, garlic, ranch, blue cheese.

Dinner

Smoked Brisket

Served with a side of choice.

Sides: coleslaw, potato salad, mashed potatoes, mac & cheese.

Flatbread Pizza

Choice of cheese, pepperoni, three-meat, or supreme.

Pork Chops

Grilled pork chops seasoned with honey mustard, garlic, and black pepper.
